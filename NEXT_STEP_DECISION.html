4. My stance

Order: A → B → C.

A is 20 minutes and closes a "probably right" hole — do it first, cheap.
B is the highest-leverage move per hour. It's the exact mechanism that let AIPSS drift to 2.25x. Writing it into YAML converts tribal knowledge into enforced policy. Do it second.
C is real engineering and should wait until a deal actually needs multi-affiliate precision. Today's ±0.02 fuzz is inside tolerance. Defer.

If you only pick one: B. It's the only one that prevents future fiction.
