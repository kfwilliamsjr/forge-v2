### Revenue Stream 2: SBA Loan Brokerage (Commission)

```
Lead source (BizBuySell, broker referral, website, outreach)
        ↓
Deal Screener skill → pass/fail/conditional verdict
        ↓
If pass: document request to borrower (checklist + context notes)
        ↓
Documents received → Financial Analyst skill extracts numbers
        ↓
G8WAY engine calculates DSCR, collateral, risk
        ↓
ClearPath dashboard updated
        ↓
Match to lender (Colony Bank or other)
        ↓
Submit deal package to lender
        ↓
Approval/conditions/decline
        ↓
If approved: close → collect 1–3% commission
```

**Current state:** Pipeline seeded with 4 test deals. No live broker deals yet.
**Commission schedule:** 1% (non-RE), 2% (RE deals), 3% (full-service UW + packaging)
