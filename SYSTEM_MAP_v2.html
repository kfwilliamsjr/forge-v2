1. Architecture at a glance

Every request enters through route_task. The router picks a lane (CIF / SBA-Broker / Prospecting / Comms) and walks the deal through 6 tiers of atomic skills. Policy YAMLs are the only source of thresholds. underwriteos is the deterministic math backend — the LLM does not calculate DSCR.
