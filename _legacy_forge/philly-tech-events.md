## Recommended for Keith

### 1. Philadelphia Business Summit & Expo + Pitch Competition — May 17–19 (Apply by Apr 23!)
**Why:** This is the City of Philadelphia Department of Commerce's flagship business event. The pitch competition on May 19 offers 3-minute pitches to judges — and selected businesses receive *funding*. ClearPath is exactly the kind of Philly-based startup this is designed for. You must be registered/licensed with the City and have an EIN. The application deadline is **April 23 at 11:59 PM** — this is 13 days away. Apply now: [Application](https://www.phila.gov/2026-04-09-philadelphia-businesses-can-apply-by-april-23-to-compete-in-the-business-summit-and-expo-pitch-competition/). Even if you don't pitch, the 3-day expo is prime territory for minority business networking and city government relationships that become distribution channels.

### 2. Philly Data & AI Meetup — Mon, Apr 21
**Why:** Highest-signal AI/data room in Philly. No slides, no pitch decks — real conversations with people building AI/ML products. Pressure-test your ClearPath automation stack, recruit engineering talent, find technical collaborators. Free, low friction, 2-hour commitment with disproportionate upside. [Register](https://www.meetup.com/philly-data-and-ai/)

### 3. SBA National Small Business Week Virtual Summit — May 5–6
**Why:** You're building *for* SBA lenders. This is where SBA leadership announces priorities, policy shifts, and lending trends that directly shape ClearPath's roadmap. Administrator Loeffler is doing a PA roadshow stop. Free. Non-negotiable for strategic intelligence and the district-office relationships that become distribution channels. [Register](https://www.sba.gov/national-small-business-week)

### 4. Technical.ly Builders Conference — May 6–8
**Why:** 300+ Philly ecosystem attendees, applied-AI workshops, founder fishbowls, live podcast recordings, and Technical.ly's editorial team in the room. Best regional brand-building and media opportunity for a Philly-based founder this quarter. Getting ClearPath on Technical.ly's radar = organic press. Worth the $55–$269 ticket. [Tickets](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/technically-builders-conference-2026-tickets-1859321884809)

### 5. Philly Tech Week — Diversitech Connect + Full Week — May 4–8
**Why:** Diversitech Connect (May 7) specifically targets diverse founders and tech professionals — directly relevant for minority business networking. The broader Tech Week umbrella hosts 100+ events across AI, startups, and fintech. Cherry-pick the sessions most relevant to lending automation and AI tooling. [Diversitech](https://luma.com/4cm7kh2w) | [Full Calendar](https://luma.com/phillytechweek)

### Bonus: Philly Builds AI — Enterprise Sherpas Fireside Chat (April, TBD)
**Why:** Specifically designed for MVP and growth-stage tech founders, sponsored by Ben Franklin Technology Partners. Ben Franklin is the most important ecosystem gatekeeper in PA — grants, introductions, credibility. A relationship here pays dividends for years. Watch for the date.
